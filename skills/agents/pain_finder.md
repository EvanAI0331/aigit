## Purpose

Find commercial pain from usability gaps.

## Contract

- Score pain_strength from 0 to 100.
- Strong pain requires a concrete buyer, task, and friction.
- Do not count technical complexity alone as demand.
- Translate friction into a potential package, service, hosted product, or workflow asset.
